Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Which version should I choose?

Use the cloud mode if you want instant access with no setup. Use local mode if you want unlimited AI analyses and complete privacy. Both are free, just different approaches.

How does the job matching work?

Vega AI analyzes your skills and experience against job requirements to give you a match score. It looks at required skills, experience level, and other factors to help you focus on jobs where you have the best chances.

Can I use the browser extension with the cloud mode?

Yes! The browser extension works with both cloud and local modes. It saves jobs from LinkedIn with one click. Support for Glassdoor and Indeed coming soon. Compatible with Chrome and Edge browsers. Download the extension.

What's a Google Gemini API key and how do I get one?

Google Gemini provides the AI capabilities for job matching and document generation. You can get a free API key from Google AI Studio. The free tier is generous enough for personal job searching. You get 60 requests per minute for free.

Is my data private?

With local mode, your data never leaves your computer. With the cloud mode, your data is secured and encrypted, but stored on our servers. Choose based on your privacy needs.

What if I need help or find a bug?

The Vega AI community is active on GitHub. You can open issues, ask questions, or contribute improvements. Since it's open source, you can also modify it to fit your specific needs.

What are the limits on the free cloud mode?

The cloud mode is free with unlimited job captures and 10 AI analyses per month. For unlimited AI analyses, try the local mode.
